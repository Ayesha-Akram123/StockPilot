Registering as a Pilot

All strategies are considered ranked by the Stock Pilot platform.Any newly registering pilots or strategies will be prompted to provide historical trade performance and all performance of on-platform strategies will be tracked.

Pre-Flight Ranking

All registering pilots are required to upload a CSV representing their personal transaction history from their brokerages.This data is used to determine a'Pre-Flight Ranking' before the strategy is published, representing a comparison to a typical investment options seen in the wild.

Platform Rankings

The platform rankings represent the on-platform performance of the strategy reletive to competing strategies by classifying the overall performance into one of three classifications: First Class, Buisness, and Economy.
